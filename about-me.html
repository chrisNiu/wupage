About Me

Wesley is a proud life-long resident of Vancouver, British Columbia. He brings to the team a Bachelor of Commerce (Real Estate) Degree and real estate appraisal experience. He uses his confidence, credibility, and extensive knowledge in Vancouver real estate market to help his clients whether they are in the buying or selling process. Being fluent in English, Cantonese, and Mandarin, Wesley is able to harmonize Eastern and Western culture.

Outside from work, I am an avid athlete and enjoys playing team sports such as soccer, hockey, and basketball. My hobbies include reading, personal growth, investing in real estate, and travelling the world.

We look forward to working with you. If you have any questions feel free to call or e-mail.
